Chicken Pesto Pasta

Description :

Chicken Pesto Pasta is a quick, easy and creamy pasta loaded with juicy chicken and tender asparagus!

Ingredients :

  • Chicken Breast
  • Asparagus
  • Basil Pesto
  • Pasta (any of the following) :
    • Fettuccini noodles
    • Linguine pasta
    • Spaghetti
    • Penne pasta
    • Pappardelle
    • Rigatoni
  • Whipped Cream
  • Any vegetables of your choice (green preferably)

Steps :

  1. The general rule of thumb for reheating a creamy sauce is LOW and SLOW
  2. Warm the pasta on low heat, stirring often or in short intervals in the microwave
  3. If you overheat, the cream can separate and seem oily
  4. To make the sauce creamier, add a splash of water, milk, half and half or cream before heating.
